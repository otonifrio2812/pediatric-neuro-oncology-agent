# Pediatric Neuro-Oncology Seed Knowledge

This local seed file is included so the RAG pipeline can run even before the autonomous evidence updater fetches PubMed and ClinicalTrials.gov records.

Important principles:
- Pediatric brain tumor planning should be conducted through a multidisciplinary tumor board.
- AI-generated imaging observations are auxiliary and require radiology / neurosurgery review.
- Brainstem, thalamic, posterior fossa, and spinal cord tumors require special attention to neurologic morbidity, cranial nerve risk, hydrocephalus, and biopsy/resection trade-offs.
- Diffuse midline glioma often requires molecular confirmation and trial evaluation; AI should not make a definitive diagnosis or treatment order.
- Surgical goals such as gross total resection versus subtotal resection must be individualized by treating clinicians.
